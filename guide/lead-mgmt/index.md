# 🎯 Lead Management  
<img width="725" alt="Lead Management Overview" src="/content/static/lead/lead.jpg">  

## Orchestrate Your Sales Pipeline from Capture to Conversion  
**Lead Management** is your comprehensive system for acquiring, tracking, and nurturing potential customers.  
Transform your sales process by centralizing lead data, automating capture via custom forms, and tracking the exact source of every prospect. With a clear view of your sales stages and campaign performance, you can ensure no opportunity falls through the cracks.  

---

## 🌟 Key Features  

### 1. Dashboard  
Get a high-level bird's-eye view of your entire sales funnel.  
The Lead Management Dashboard provides **real-time metrics** on lead volume, conversion rates, and pipeline velocity. Monitor which campaigns are performing best and identify bottlenecks in your sales stages at a glance.

---

### 2. Leads  
The "Source of Truth" for every prospect in your ecosystem.  
- **Unified Storage** → Every lead captured is stored in a structured, searchable database.  
- **Lead Profiles** → View detailed history, contact information, and the specific journey each lead has taken.  
- **Management** → Easily filter, sort, and update lead information as they move through your funnel.

---

### 3. Fields & Forms  
**The Engine of Lead Capture.** - **Custom Fields** → Define the specific data points your business needs (e.g., Budget, Industry, Preferences). These fields serve as the building blocks for your forms.  
- **Form Builder** → Create intuitive, high-converting forms using your custom fields. These forms can be embedded or shared to collect lead data directly into the system.

---

### 4. Campaigns  
**Precision Source Tracking.** Understand exactly where your leads are coming from. Generate unique tracking links for a single form to distinguish between different traffic sources.  
- **Multi-Channel Tracking** → Create specific links for **Instagram, WhatsApp, Email, or Offline QR codes**.  
- **Attribution** → Automatically tag incoming leads with their source campaign to measure the ROI of your marketing efforts.

---

### 5. Stages  
**Visualize Your Sales Journey.** Define the lifecycle of a lead with customizable **Stages**.  
- **Pipeline Management** → Map out the steps a lead takes, such as *New, Contacted, Qualified, Proposal Sent,* and *Closed/Resolved*.  
- **Status Tracking** → Monitor how many leads are sitting in each stage to optimize your team's follow-up strategy.

---

## 🎯 The Lead Management Advantage  
- **Zero Lead Leakage** → Centralized capture ensures every prospect is accounted for.  
- **Deep Attribution** → Know which platforms (Instagram vs. WhatsApp) actually drive revenue.  
- **Process Standardization** → Ensure every lead follows the same structured path to conversion.  
- **Data-Driven Growth** → Use conversion analytics to refine your sales pitch and marketing spend.
