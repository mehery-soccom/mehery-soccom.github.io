# 🚀 Campaigns
Campaigns are the bridge between your marketing efforts and your lead capture forms. They allow you to generate unique, trackable links for a single form so you can identify exactly which source—Instagram, WhatsApp, Email, or an Offline QR Code—is driving your business growth.

---

## 🔗 The Power of Unique Links
In Lead Management, a **Form** defines *what* data you collect, but a **Campaign** defines *where* the lead comes from. 

You can create multiple campaigns for the same form. For example:
* **Form**: "Contact Us" -> **Campaign 1**: "Insta_Bio_Link"
* **Form**: "Contact Us" -> **Campaign 2**: "WhatsApp_Broadcast"

When a lead is captured, the system automatically tags it with the Campaign Title, giving you 100% accurate attribution on your **Dashboard**.

---

## 🔑 Key Features

### 1. Link Management
Every active campaign generates a unique URL. 
* **Copy Link**: Use the **Link Icon** in the actions column to quickly copy the URL to your clipboard.
* **Status-Based Access**: The copy link feature is only available for **Active** campaigns. If a campaign is deactivated, the link will no longer function for lead capture.

### 2. Status Control (Activation/Deactivation)
Take full control over your marketing window:
* **Toggle Switch**: Use the `isActive` switch to instantly turn a campaign link on or off. 
* **Safety Confirmation**: To prevent accidental deactivations, the system requires a manual confirmation before changing the status.

### 3. Form Association
When creating a campaign, you must select one of your existing **Forms**. 
* **Form Locking**: Once a campaign is created, the associated form is locked (disabled for editing). This ensures that the data attribution for that campaign remains consistent over time.

---

## 🛠️ Creating a Campaign
The creation process is designed for speed:

1.  **Campaign Title**: Provide a clear name that identifies the source (e.g., "Summer Sale 2026 - FB").
2.  **Description**: Add internal notes about the target audience or the specific goal of this link.
3.  **Select Form**: Choose the lead capture form you want the prospect to fill out. 
4.  **Auto-Generation**: Once saved, the system automatically generates the unique tracking link using the `formCode`.

---

## 🚀 Pro-Tips for Attribution
* **Naming Conventions**: Use a consistent naming pattern for your campaigns (e.g., `Platform_Month_CampaignName`) to make your Dashboard analytics easier to read.
* **Deactivate Old Links**: If a seasonal promotion ends, deactivate the campaign. This prevents "stale" leads from entering your pipeline and keeps your analytics clean.
* **Test the Link**: Always use the **Copy Link** feature and open it in an incognito window to ensure the branding and fields look perfect before you spend money on ads.
