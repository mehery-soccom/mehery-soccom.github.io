# 👥 Leads Management
Manage your prospects effectively with a centralized list and detailed lead tracking. The Leads module allows you to move beyond simple data storage to active engagement through integrated chat, team assignment, and pipeline tracking.

<img width="725" alt="Leads List View" src="/content/static/lead/leadList.png">

---

## 📋 Lead List Overview
The main leads table provides a bird's-eye view of your current pipeline. It supports server-side pagination and real-time filtering to handle high volumes of data efficiently.

### 🔑 Key Actions
1. **Bulk Assignment**: Admins and Managers can select multiple leads and assign them to a specific Agent/Moderator in one click.
2. **Instant Communication**: Click the **Chat Icon** on any lead to open Mehery conversation window. The system automatically encodes lead data (Name, Phone, Email) to initiate a seamless chat experience.
3. **Manual Creation**: Use the **"Create Lead"** button to manually input data for prospects gathered through offline channels or phone calls.

---

## 🔍 Lead Details & Overview
Clicking on any lead row opens a comprehensive overview. This view is divided into two main tabs: **Details** and **Timeline**.

### 1. The Details Tab
This is where the active management of a lead happens:

* **Lead Progress (Stages)**: Visualize where the lead stands in the sales funnel. You can update the stage, set an expected **Closing Date**, and reassign the lead to a different team member.
* **Dynamic Data Engine**: The system renders fields dynamically based on the form the lead originated from. It supports:
    * **Validated Inputs**: Phone numbers with country codes, emails, and required fields.
    * **Document Management**: Upload and view specific documents (PDFs, Images) required for lead qualification.
    * **Field-Level Permissions**: Field visibility and "Read-Only" status adjust automatically based on user roles.
* **Activity Logging**: Record follow-ups, notes, and specific interactions to ensure every team member is updated on the lead's status.

### 2. The Timeline Tab
Total transparency of the lead's journey.
* **History Tracking**: View every change made to the lead, including stage transitions, field updates, and assignment changes.
* **Audit Trail**: See exactly who made a change and when it happened, ensuring accountability across the sales team.

---

## 🛠 Manual Lead Creation
When creating a lead manually, the process is streamlined:
1.  **Select a Form**: Choose a predefined form to automatically generate the necessary input fields.
2.  **Smart Fill**: If you are coming from a specific referral link, the system can auto-populate known parameters (like name or phone number).
3.  **Initial Stage**: All manual leads are automatically placed in the "Initial" stage to begin their journey through your pipeline.

---

## 🚀 Pro-Tips for Lead Management
* **Use the Chat Icon**: Don't wait for leads to reach out. Use the integrated chat to follow up on new leads as soon as they appear in the list.
* **Set Closing Dates**: Always set an estimated closing date to help the **Dashboard** calculate accurate pipeline velocity and revenue projections.
* **Monitor the Timeline**: If a lead is "stuck," review the timeline to see historical interactions and identify why the conversion is stalling.
