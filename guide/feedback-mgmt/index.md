# 💬 Feedback Management  
<img width="725" alt="Feedback Management Overview" src="/content/static/feedback/fm.webp">  

## Close the Loop with Actionable Customer Insights  
**Feedback Management** is your dedicated system for capturing, organizing, and resolving customer input.  
Transform raw opinions into a strategic asset by centralizing feedback data, automating collection via custom forms, and tracking every submission through to resolution. By maintaining a clear view of feedback statuses, you can ensure your customers feel heard and your business continues to evolve based on real-world evidence.

---

## 🌟 Key Features  

### 1. Dashboard  
Gain a high-level view of customer sentiment and team responsiveness.  
The Feedback Dashboard provides **real-time metrics** on submission volume, average resolution time, and satisfaction trends. Monitor which areas of your business receive the most input and track how efficiently your team is "closing the loop" on open items.

---

### 2. Feedbacks (Central Repository)  
The "Source of Truth" for every customer voice in your ecosystem.  
- **Unified Storage** → Every response captured is stored in a structured, searchable database for easy retrieval.  
- **Submission Profiles** → View detailed context, contact information, and the specific history of how each piece of feedback was handled.  
- **Resolution Tracking** → Easily filter, sort, and update feedback entries as they move from initial receipt to final resolution.

---

### 3. Fields & Forms  
**The Engine of Insight Capture.** - **Custom Fields** → Define the specific data points your business needs to measure (e.g., Rating Scales, Category, Sentiment, or Suggestion boxes). These fields serve as the building blocks for your surveys.  
- **Form Builder** → Create intuitive, branded feedback forms using your custom fields. These forms can be shared via QR codes, links, or embedded directly into your customer touchpoints.

---

### 4. Statuses  
**Visualize the Resolution Journey.** Define the lifecycle of a feedback entry with customizable **Statuses**.  
- **Workflow Management** → Map out the steps a submission takes, such as *New, Under Review, In Progress, Resolved,* or *Closed*.  
- **Accountability** → Track which status each piece of feedback is currently in to ensure timely responses and prevent important insights from being overlooked.

---

## 🎯 The Feedback Management Advantage  
- **Centralized Listening** → Stop losing feedback in emails or chats; keep everything in one accessible location.  
- **Sentiment Accuracy** → Use custom fields to quantify qualitative data, making it easier to spot business-wide trends.  
- **Improved Customer Loyalty** → Turning feedback into action proves to your customers that their input directly impacts your business.  
- **Data-Driven Evolution** → Use historical feedback data to justify product changes, service improvements, or operational shifts.
