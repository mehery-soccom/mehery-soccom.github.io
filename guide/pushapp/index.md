# PushApp

Welcome to PushApp 👋

PushApp is your all-in-one platform to **engage, analyze, and grow your user base** — without needing engineering support.

With PushApp, you can:

- 📲 Reach users instantly with push notifications
- 💬 Engage users inside your app with in-app experiences
- 👤 Understand users through profiles, cohorts, and behavior tracking
- 📊 Analyze user actions with powerful event analytics

Whether you're launching campaigns, bringing users back, or optimizing conversions — PushApp helps you take action with confidence.
