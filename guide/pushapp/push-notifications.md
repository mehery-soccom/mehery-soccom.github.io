# Push Notifications

## 🧾 Overview

Push notifications allow you to reach your users instantly on their devices — even when they are not actively using your app.

They are one of the most effective ways to:

- Bring users back to your app
- Promote offers and updates
- Drive conversions and engagement

---

## 🎯 When should you use Push Notifications?

Use push notifications when you want to:

- Re-engage inactive users
- Remind users to complete an action
- Announce offers, updates, or new features
- Send time-sensitive alerts

💡 Tip: The best push notifications are timely, relevant, and action-driven.

## 🚀 Create a Push Campaign

Push notifications are sent through **campaigns**.

### Step 1: Navigate to Push Campaigns

Go to:

Campaigns → Push Notifications → **Create Campaign**

<APP IMAGES HERE>

---

### Step 2: Select Your Audience

Choose who should receive the notification:

- All Users
- Specific Cohorts
- Custom filters (based on events or attributes)

💡 Tip: Targeted campaigns perform significantly better than sending to all users.

---

### Step 3: Create Your Message / Select Template

Define what users will see:

- **Title** → Short and attention-grabbing
- **Message** → Clear and actionable
- Optional: Image / icon

💡 Best Practices:

- Keep it concise (1–2 lines)
- Use action-oriented language (e.g. “Shop Now”, “Complete Your Order”)

### Step 4: Choose When to Send

You can:

- Send immediately
- Schedule for later
- Set recurring campaigns

💡 Tip: Send notifications when your users are most active for better engagement.

---

### Step 5: Review & Launch

Before sending:

- Verify your audience
- Preview your message
- Confirm timing

Click **Launch Campaign** 🚀

---

## 📊 Track Performance

After sending a campaign, monitor:

- Delivery rate → Were notifications received?
- Open rate → Did users engage?
- Conversions → Did users take action?

## ⚠️ Common Mistakes

❌ Sending too many notifications → leads to opt-outs  
❌ Generic messaging → low engagement  
❌ Poor timing → notifications ignored  
❌ Not using cohorts → irrelevant targeting
